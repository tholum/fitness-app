Fitness App — Three Looks & Feels

Same app, same MTNTOUGH program + body/nutrition data — three wildly different design philosophies. Tap through each phone (Today · Train · Body · Progress · Look) to feel the difference. The Look tab in each shows how that variant's appearance gets configured.

★ Your direction · SUMMIT colors × FORGE layout

04 · BASECAMP

The merge you asked for: SUMMIT's earthy blaze/moss palette and rugged type on FORGE's gamified, card-driven layout — rebuilt around completion-first tracking (follow the MTNTOUGH video, then check it off) and a Crew tab for accountability.

  • Today → "Watch on MTNTOUGH" + "Check In", plus crew-today strip
  • Check In → tick off session blocks, optional weight/time logging, share to crew
  • Crew → weekly leaderboard, activity feed, reactions & nudges
  • Body · Progress · Look → fuel rings, streaks, badges, config
